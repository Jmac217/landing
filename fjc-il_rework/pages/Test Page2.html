Welcome

Welcome to the First Judicial Circuit of Illinois.  The First Circuit consists of the southern nine counties in the  State of Illinois.  These are the counties of Alexander, Jackson, Johnson,  Massac, Pope, Pulaski, Saline, Union, and Williamson.
